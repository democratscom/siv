Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Information We Collect

When you sign a petition, pledge, or form on this site, we collect your name, email address, zip code, and optionally your phone number. We use this information to send you updates about the Stop ICE campaign, upcoming elections, and opportunities to take action.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Send you updates, news, and calls to action from the Stop ICE campaign
  • Contact you about candidates we support and elections you can influence
  • Report petition signatures and supporter counts

We may share your information with Democrats.com and partner organizations for campaign purposes, consistent with the purpose for which you signed up. For full details on how Democrats.com handles personal data, see the Democrats.com Privacy Policy.

Cookies & Analytics

This site uses cookies to understand how visitors use it and to improve your experience. We may use Google Analytics or similar tools to track aggregate, anonymized usage data. You can opt out of analytics cookies by declining the cookie consent banner when you first visit the site.

Action Network, our petition platform, may set its own cookies. See actionnetwork.org/privacy for their policy.

Your Rights

You may unsubscribe from our emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send. To request that we delete your personal data, contact us here.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including the right to know what personal information we have collected, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale. For details on how data is handled, see the Democrats.com Privacy Policy. To exercise your rights, contact us here.
